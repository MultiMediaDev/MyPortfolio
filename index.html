About Me

I’m a multimedia development technician driven by a deep passion for digital creation, visual storytelling, and knowledge sharing.
With a background in economics and environmental systems, I’ve always been fascinated by the intersection of people, technology, and ideas.

My journey began in data analysis—unpacking complex concepts—and evolved into translating those ideas through images, sound, and interactivity.
Today, I blend graphic design, web development, and audiovisual production to craft experiences that inform, inspire, and connect.

Whether I’m designing visual identities, building interactive learning tools, or producing dynamic multimedia content, I aim to fuse creativity, precision, and purpose in every project.

My roles as a trainer and IT manager taught me the importance of structure, clarity, and storytelling—the kind that stays with people.
I see myself as a hybrid creative, standing at the crossroads of tech, education, and visual communication—shaping meaningful content that resonates.
